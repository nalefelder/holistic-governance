Frequently Asked Questions

What types of organisations do you work with?
We work primarily with aged care providers, hospitals, day surgery centres, disability services, community health organisations, and digital health companies. We also support boards and executive teams across the broader health and human services sector.
How long does a typical engagement last?
It depends on the scope. A focused audit or gap analysis might take 2–4 weeks. Governance framework builds and accreditation readiness projects typically run 3–6 months. We also offer ongoing retainer arrangements for organisations that need sustained support.
How is pricing structured?
We offer project-based fixed pricing, day rates, and retainer packages depending on the engagement. Every proposal includes a clear scope and transparent pricing — no hidden fees. Request a proposal for a tailored quote.
Do you work on-site or remotely?
Both. Many engagements are delivered remotely, but we attend on-site for audits, workshops, accreditation support, and any work that benefits from being embedded with your team. We're based in Victoria and work with organisations across Australia, Tasmania, and New Zealand.
What makes you different from larger consultancies?
You work directly with a senior consultant — not a junior associate. We bring deep clinical and governance expertise rather than generic management frameworks. We deliberately limit the number of clients we take on to ensure focused, high-quality support. And we build systems you own and can sustain independently.
